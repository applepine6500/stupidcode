Sustainable Solutions:

As a consecutive yearly winner of the national Michelin food competition, we, Spice and Spark Grill try to make our industrialisation and production methods as sustainable as possible. First, we grow our own crops, making it purely organic to both the environment and our lovely animals. Growing crops can have beneficial purposes to life, whether we're talking about nutrients, etc. Second, we encourage our loyal customers to create less and less waste each time they consume our delicious meals. As the restaurant prepares fresh food everyday, we try to minimalise our usage rate, selling the excess to donations, ergo the people in need. We also manage our electrical/water use/rate, hoping to have an estimate of reducing the bill and fees by 5% each year. Lastly, we try to compel and explore different food options instead of using meat, for example. We try to make vegan alternatives as tasty as the food we make, applying environmental components to our food too. This is used to protect the endangered animals our world is facing, as many things are killed for simple things that can be replaced with many alternatives. So, based on the exterior you see for our restaurant, no animal products are used but we use recycable plastic and wood..

At our restaurant, we prioritize sustainability by sourcing food and ingredients from local farms that use organic practices, making sure its fresh, seasonal produce while reducing our carbon footprint. Our seafood comes exclusively from fisheries that follow sustainable practices, and we choose meat and dairy from suppliers that are commited to animal welfare and humane farming. By designing seasonal menus, we make the best of local harvest, minimize waste. Our active stepts to reduce climate change in the community helps change climate change one resturant at a time.
